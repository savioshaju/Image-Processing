Privacy & Ethics

Our application is designed with privacy and ethical considerations at its core:

  • All processing occurs locally on the user's device with no external data transmission
  • Human detection uses a conservative approach to ensure no identities are accidentally revealed
  • Blurring techniques are irreversible to prevent de-anonymization attempts
  • No personal data is stored or logged during the processing workflow
  • The system is designed to comply with privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA
